This ROM hack includes several quality of life improvements to make training and evolving Pokémon much easier!

---

## 🎯 **Why These Changes?**

These quality of life improvements allow you to:
1. **Focus on battling** instead of grinding
2. **Experiment with different teams** without time investment
3. **Evolve Pokémon easily** without hunting for rare items
4. **Train for post-game content** like Gym Leader rematches
5. **Complete your Pokédex** more efficiently

---

## 💡 **Tips:**

### **Rare Candy Strategy:**
- Buy in bulk! At only 10 Pokedollars each, stock up early
- Use for Pokémon that evolve at high levels (Dragonite at 55, Tyranitar at 55)
- Perfect for evolving friendship-based Pokémon after they're happy
- Great for quickly leveling Pokémon to learn specific moves

### **Evolution Items Strategy:**
- Visit Goldenrod Department Store 4F early in your journey
- Stock up on stones you'll need for your team
- Don't worry about "wasting" stones - you can always buy more!
- Experiment with different Eevee evolutions

### **Money Making:**
- Battle trainers for money
- Sell Nuggets (found throughout the game)
- Use Amulet Coin to double prize money
- Rematch Gym Leaders for good prize money
