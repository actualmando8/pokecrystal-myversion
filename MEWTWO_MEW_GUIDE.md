# Mewtwo & Mew - Legendary Encounter Guide

The ultimate legendary Pokémon, **Mewtwo** and **Mew**, are now available in realistic, challenging locations that fit their lore perfectly.

---

## 🧬 **Mewtwo - The Genetic Pokémon**

**Type:** Psychic  
**Level:** 70  
**Location:** **Mt. Silver - Room 3** (Same room as Red)

### **How to Find Mewtwo:**

1. **Obtain all 16 Gym Badges** (8 Johto + 8 Kanto)
2. **Reach Mt. Silver** - The ultimate challenge location
3. **Navigate through Mt. Silver Cave** to the deepest chamber (Room 3)
4. **Before facing Red**, you'll encounter Mewtwo in the chamber
5. **Save before battling!** - This is a one-time encounter

### **Thematic Reasoning:**
- **Mt. Silver is the ultimate location** - Only the strongest trainers reach it
- **Guarding the path to Red** - Two legendary encounters in one place
- **Level 70** - The highest level wild Pokémon, fitting for the strongest legendary
- **Psychic power** - The mental energy of Mt. Silver's peak attracts Mewtwo
- **Isolation** - Mewtwo seeks the most remote location to avoid humans

### **Battle Strategy:**
- **Recommended Level:** 65-70 for a fair fight
- **Bring Master Ball** - Or save it for Mew
- **Use Dark-types** - Immune to Psychic moves
- **Status moves** - Sleep or Paralysis greatly helps
- **Stock Ultra Balls** - Bring 50+ if not using Master Ball

---

## 🎀 **Mew - The New Species Pokémon**

**Type:** Psychic  
**Level:** 30  
**Location:** **Ruins of Alph - Inner Chamber**

### **How to Find Mew:**

1. **Solve all four Ruins of Alph puzzles:**
   - Kabuto Chamber
   - Omanyte Chamber
   - Aerodactyl Chamber
   - Ho-Oh Chamber

2. **Enter the Inner Chamber** (center of the ruins)
3. **Mew will appear** floating in the chamber
4. **Save before battling!** - This is a one-time encounter

### **Thematic Reasoning:**
- **Ancient ruins** - Mew is said to be the ancestor of all Pokémon
- **Mysterious location** - The Ruins of Alph are filled with ancient secrets
- **Unown connection** - Mew and Unown share mysterious origins
- **Level 30** - Accessible earlier, but still challenging to catch
- **Hidden discovery** - Requires solving all puzzles to unlock

### **Battle Strategy:**
- **Recommended Level:** 30-35 for a fair fight
- **Bring Master Ball** - Mew is extremely rare and difficult to catch
- **Use False Swipe** - Get it to 1 HP safely
- **Status moves** - Sleep is most effective
- **Timer Balls** - The battle can take a while
- **Quick Balls** - Try on turn 1 for a lucky catch

## 💡 **Catching Tips**

### **For Mewtwo (Level 70):**
1. **Save your Master Ball** - Mewtwo has the lowest catch rate
2. **Bring Dark-types** - Umbreon, Houndoom, Tyranitar are immune to Psychic
3. **Use Dusk Balls** - Mt. Silver cave gives bonus catch rate
4. **Paralyze, don't sleep** - Mewtwo knows Recover and can heal
5. **Be patient** - This can take 50+ Ultra Balls

### **For Mew (Level 30):**
1. **Use Master Ball if you have it** - Mew is incredibly rare
2. **False Swipe to 1 HP** - Mew is fragile at level 30
3. **Put it to sleep** - Most effective status condition
4. **Use Timer Balls** - After 10 turns, they're better than Ultra Balls
5. **Quick Ball first turn** - 5x catch rate on turn 1

### **Mew (Psychic):**
- **Weak to:** Bug, Ghost, Dark
- **Resistant to:** Fighting, Psychic
- **Immune to:** Nothing
- **Best counters:** Umbreon, Murkrow, Sneasel, Ariados

## 📖 **Lore Integration**

### **Mewtwo's Presence:**
Mewtwo, the ultimate creation of genetic engineering, has fled to the most remote location in the world - the peak of Mt. Silver. Here, it guards the path to Red, the legendary trainer. Only those who prove themselves worthy by reaching this sacred peak may challenge the most powerful Pokémon ever created.

### **Mew's Presence:**
Mew, the ancestor of all Pokémon, has been drawn to the Ruins of Alph by the ancient energy of the Unown. The ruins, built by an ancient civilization that worshipped Pokémon, resonate with Mew's primordial power. Only those who solve all four ancient puzzles and prove their wisdom may encounter this mythical being.

## 📝 **Notes**

- Both Mewtwo and Mew are **one-time encounters**
- If you defeat them without catching, they will **not respawn** (save before battling!)
- **Master Ball recommended** for at least one of them
- Mewtwo is significantly harder to catch due to its level and stats
- Mew appears after solving all Ruins of Alph puzzles (no specific event flag needed)
- Both can be shiny hunted with soft resets

---

## 🎮 **Recommended Catching Order**

1. **Catch Mew first** (Level 30, Ruins of Alph)
   - Use Ultra Balls and status moves
   - Save Master Ball for Mewtwo

2. **Catch Mewtwo last** (Level 70, Mt. Silver)
   - Use Master Ball for guaranteed catch
   - Or prepare for a long battle with 50+ Ultra Balls

This ensures you have the best chance of catching both legendary Pokémon!
