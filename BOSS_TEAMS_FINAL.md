# Boss Teams Updated - Final (Thematically Appropriate Only)

All major boss teams have been updated with **only thematically appropriate** new Pokemon and moves.

- Silver already uses Sneasel and Magneton throughout the entire game
- Evolving them to **Weavile** and **Magnezone** is natural progression
- These are his signature Pokemon, just fully evolved

## 🎯 **Philosophy Applied**

**Only added new Pokemon when:**
1. ✅ They're natural evolutions of Pokemon the trainer already uses
2. ✅ They fit the trainer's type specialty/theme
3. ✅ They make sense for that character's story/personality

**Did NOT add new Pokemon when:**
1. ❌ They're just strong but don't fit the theme
2. ❌ They provide coverage but break type specialty
3. ❌ They're new but don't make thematic sense
