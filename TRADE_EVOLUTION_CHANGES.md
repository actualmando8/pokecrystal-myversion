# Trade Evolution Removal - Complete List

All trade evolutions have been removed and replaced with level-up or item-based evolutions to make all Pokémon obtainable in single-player.

## Changes Made:

### **Level-Up Evolutions (Trade → Level)**

| Pokémon | Old Method | New Method | Level |
|---------|-----------|------------|-------|
| **Kadabra** → Alakazam | Trade | Level Up | **37** |
| **Machoke** → Machamp | Trade | Level Up | **37** |
| **Graveler** → Golem | Trade | Level Up | **37** |
| **Haunter** → Gengar | Trade | Level Up | **37** |
| **Electabuzz** → Electivire | Trade | Level Up | **42** |
| **Magmar** → Magmortar | Trade | Level Up | **42** |
| **Rhydon** → Rhyperior | Trade | Level Up | **42** |
| **Porygon2** → Porygon-Z | Trade | Level Up | **42** |

### **Item-Based Evolutions (Trade with Item → Use Item)**

| Pokémon | Old Method | New Method | Item |
|---------|-----------|------------|------|
| **Slowpoke** → Slowking | Trade holding King's Rock | Use Item | **King's Rock** |
| **Poliwhirl** → Poliwrath | Trade with Water Stone | Use Item | **Water Stone** |
| **Poliwhirl** → Politoed | Trade holding King's Rock | Use Item | **King's Rock** |
| **Onix** → Steelix | Trade holding Metal Coat | Use Item | **Metal Coat** |
| **Seadra** → Kingdra | Trade holding Dragon Scale | Use Item | **Dragon Scale** |
| **Scyther** → Scizor | Trade holding Metal Coat | Use Item | **Metal Coat** |
| **Porygon** → Porygon2 | Trade holding Up-Grade | Use Item | **Up-Grade** |

## Evolution Levels Explained:

- **Level 37**: Standard trade evolutions (Alakazam, Machamp, Golem, Gengar)
  - Chosen to match typical mid-game progression
  - Slightly later than their pre-evolution to maintain balance

- **Level 42**: Late-game evolutions (Electivire, Magmortar, Rhyperior, Porygon-Z)
  - These are powerful Pokémon that evolve in late-game
  - Accessible before Elite Four but still challenging to obtain
  - Balanced for competitive endgame content

## Benefits:

✅ **All Pokémon obtainable in single-player** - No trading required
✅ **Balanced progression** - Evolution levels match game difficulty
✅ **Item evolutions preserved** - Special items still have purpose
✅ **No gameplay exploits** - Can't get overpowered Pokémon too early

## Notes:

- Evolution items (King's Rock, Metal Coat, Dragon Scale, Up-Grade) can be found in-game or purchased
- All evolution methods are now accessible without needing a second game or trading partner
- Pokémon that previously required trading can now be completed in the Pokédex solo
