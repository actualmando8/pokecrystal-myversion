# Johto Gym Leader Rematches

After defeating the Elite Four and becoming Champion, you can now **rematch all 8 Johto Gym Leaders** with their **stronger, upgraded teams**!

---

## 🎯 **How It Works**

- **Requirement:** Beat the Elite Four (have the Rising Badge from Lance)
- **Trigger:** Return to any Johto Gym and talk to the Gym Leader
- **Teams:** Each leader has a Level 54-58 team with held items and competitive movesets
- **Repeatable:** You can battle them as many times as you want!

## 💡 **Tips for Rematches**

- **Save before battling** - These teams are tough!
- **Bring type counters** - Each leader specializes in one type
- **Use held items** - Leaders now use held items, so should you
- **Level 60+ recommended** - These teams are post-Elite Four difficulty
- **Exploit weaknesses** - Flying weak to Electric/Ice, Bug weak to Fire/Flying, etc.
