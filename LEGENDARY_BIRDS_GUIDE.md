# Legendary Bird Trio - Encounter Guide

The legendary bird trio (Articuno, Zapdos, and Moltres) are now available in **realistic, thematic locations** throughout Johto and Kanto.

---

## 🐦 **Legendary Bird Locations**

### **❄️ Articuno - The Freeze Pokémon**
**Type:** Ice/Flying  
**Level:** 50  
**Location:** **Ice Path B3F** (Deepest chamber)

**How to Find:**
1. Navigate through Ice Path from Mahogany Town or Blackthorn City
2. Reach the deepest level (B3F)
3. Articuno will be standing in the back of the cave
4. **One-time encounter** - Save before battling!

**Thematic Reasoning:**
- Ice Path is the coldest location in Johto
- Deep underground ice caves are perfect for an Ice-type legendary
- Fits the lore of Articuno dwelling in icy mountains

---

### **⚡ Zapdos - The Electric Pokémon**
**Type:** Electric/Flying  
**Level:** 50  
**Location:** **Route 10 (Kanto)** - Power Plant area

**How to Find:**
1. Travel to Kanto after beating the Elite Four
2. Go to Route 10 (between Lavender Town and Rock Tunnel)
3. Near the Power Plant entrance
4. Zapdos will be standing on the rocky outcrop
5. **One-time encounter** - Save before battling!

**Thematic Reasoning:**
- Power Plant is the most electric-themed location
- Route 10 has the abandoned power facility
- Matches Zapdos's association with electricity and thunderstorms

---

### **🔥 Moltres - The Flame Pokémon**
**Type:** Fire/Flying  
**Level:** 50  
**Location:** **Mt. Silver Exterior** (Outside the cave entrance)

**How to Find:**
1. Reach Mt. Silver (requires all 16 badges)
2. Before entering the cave, check the mountain path
3. Moltres will be perched on the mountainside
4. **One-time encounter** - Save before battling!

**Thematic Reasoning:**
- Mt. Silver is the highest peak in the region
- Volcanic mountain setting fits Moltres's Fire typing
- Legendary location for a legendary Pokémon
- Guarding the path to Red (another legendary encounter)

---

## 📋 **Encounter Details**

### **All Three Birds:**
- **Level:** 50 (challenging but fair for post-Elite Four)
- **Encounter Type:** Static (one-time, respawns if you KO them and beat Elite Four again)
- **Flee Rate:** High (save before battling!)
- **Shiny-locked:** No (can be shiny hunted with soft resets)

### **Recommended Team Level:**
- **45-50** for a fair challenge
- **50+** for easier capture

### **Best Poké Balls:**
- **Ultra Balls** - Standard choice
- **Dusk Balls** - Excellent in caves (Articuno)
- **Timer Balls** - For long battles
- **Fast Balls** - For their high Speed stats

---

## 💡 **Catching Tips**

### **General Strategy:**
1. **Save before the encounter** - Legendary birds can flee or be KO'd
2. **Bring False Swipe** - Get them to 1 HP safely
3. **Use status moves** - Sleep or Paralysis helps significantly
4. **Stock up on balls** - Bring 50+ Ultra Balls minimum
5. **Bring a Pokémon with Mean Look** - Prevents fleeing

### **Type Advantages:**

**Articuno (Ice/Flying):**
- Weak to: Rock, Steel, Fire, Electric
- Resistant to: Grass, Bug
- Use Rock-type moves for 4x damage

**Zapdos (Electric/Flying):**
- Weak to: Rock, Ice
- Resistant to: Fighting, Flying, Bug, Steel, Grass
- Use Rock-type moves for 4x damage

**Moltres (Fire/Flying):**
- Weak to: Rock, Water, Electric
- Resistant to: Fighting, Bug, Steel, Fire, Grass
- Use Rock-type moves for 4x damage

## ⚙️ **Implementation Details**

The legendary birds have been added as **static encounters** in the following map files:

1. **Articuno:** `maps/IcePathB3F.asm`
2. **Zapdos:** `maps/Route10North.asm` (near Power Plant)
3. **Moltres:** `maps/SilverCaveOutside.asm`

Each bird:
- Appears as an overworld sprite
- Initiates battle when interacted with
- Has appropriate event flags to prevent re-encounters
- Can be re-battled if defeated (after beating Elite Four again)

## 📝 **Lore Integration**

The legendary birds have been drawn to the region by the presence of other legendary Pokémon (Lugia, Ho-Oh, and the legendary beasts). Each has claimed a territory that matches their elemental affinity:

- **Articuno** seeks the coldest depths of Ice Path
- **Zapdos** is attracted to the electromagnetic energy of the Power Plant
- **Moltres** roosts on the volcanic Mt. Silver, the highest peak

This creates a natural progression: encounter them as you explore post-game areas, culminating in the ultimate challenge of facing Red at Mt. Silver's summit.
